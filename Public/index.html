How Kufikia Works

  • Start with a team

    Learning new technology is fun, but it's even more fun when a team is working alongside you. We group you with nine other classmates that you'll collaborate with and learn from throughout the nine week program.

  • We'll bring in sponsors

    Each cohort is sponsored by three amazing companies. You'll get to know and learn from each of them over the course of the nine weeks.

  • They'll bring along mentors

    You'll have mentors--generously provided by our sponsor companies--to give you help when you need it. They'll be there to answer questions and steer you in the right direction.

  • We'll give you lots of motivation to finish

    Learning something new on your own can be tough. With Kufikia, you'll learn with other skilled developers, and you'll get help when you need it. We'll do everything we can to make sure you're successful.
